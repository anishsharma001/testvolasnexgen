About Us

VOLAS NexGen Marine is a leading global provider of independent ship management services, with a reputation for excellence and innovation driven by our passion for seafaring and for ships.

We are committed to doing the right things the right way, always challenging ourselves to shape a better and sustainable maritime future.

We continually seek to perform at the highest standards of safety, performance and crew wellbeing in our industry, values that are core to who we are. Our numbers reflect why clients trust us with their assets.

Mission & Vision

MISSION

VOLAS NexGen Marine aims to excel in shipping, trading & services by remaining transparent, focused and technologically advanced.

To be the most reliable and trustworthy partner for our clients.

VISION

VOLAS NexGen Marine aims to be a leading shipping, trading & services provider to the maritime industry with absolute regard to safety and the environment.

The company aims to keep abreast with ever evolving technology and offer modern and dynamic maritime solutions.

Our Philosophy

  • Our emphasis on crew welfare and safety culture sets us apart. We invest heavily in training and technology to ensure our seafarers thrive, which keeps operations smooth and clients satisfied.
  • We have a solid financial position that makes us a strong ally to our customers, partners, and stakeholders.
  • We bring the knowledge and high expectations of top-notch projects on sophisticated assets to all segments and all types of vessels.
